This was an unfamiliar word — growing up, and to an extent, still. I have held, hemmed and hawed at this role for around 5 years now, and my tools and thoughts have changed a lot. I have gone from trying to solve *every* problem in people's life, to equipping them to find their own answers.

Overall, it helps to have good managers for yourself, regardless of whether you enter this role — and I have had excellent versions of them, twice before. 

I will leave you with a poem by the late Vinod Kumar Shukla


> हताशा से एक व्यक्ति बैठ गया था\
> व्यक्ति को मैं नहीं जानता था
>
> हताशा को जानता था\
> इसलिए मैं उस व्यक्ति के पास गया
>
> मैंने हाथ बढ़ाया\
> मेरा हाथ पकड़कर वह खड़ा हुआ
>
> मुझे वह नहीं जानता था\
> मेरे हाथ बढ़ाने को जानता था
>
> हम दोनों साथ चले\
> दोनों एक दूसरे को नहीं जानते थे
>
> साथ चलने को जानते थे।
